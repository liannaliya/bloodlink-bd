Universal Donor

O- (O Negative) is the universal donor. O- blood can be given to patients of any blood group in emergencies, making O- donors extremely valuable during critical situations.

Universal Recipient

AB+ (AB Positive) is the universal recipient. AB+ individuals can receive blood from any blood group, giving them the most flexibility during emergencies.

Donation Interval

WHO recommends a minimum of 90 days (3 months) between whole blood donations. BloodLink BD automatically tracks this for every registered donor.

Donor Eligibility

To donate blood you must be 18–60 years old, weigh at least 45 kg, and be in good health with no active infections or disqualifying medical conditions.
